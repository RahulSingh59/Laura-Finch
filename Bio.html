I am an Assistant Professor in the Literature Department at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and a member of the Society of Fellows at the University of Michigan (2017-2020). Previously, I held a one-year appointment as the Gillespie Visiting Assistant Professor of English at The College of Wooster. I received my Ph.D. in Comparative Literature and Literary Theory at the University of Pennsylvania in 2016, and my M.A. in Critical Theory (2008) and B.A. in English Literature (2006) from the University of Sussex.
